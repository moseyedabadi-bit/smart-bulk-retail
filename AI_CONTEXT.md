# Smart Bulk Retail - AI Context

## Project Summary

Smart Bulk Retail is a retail innovation framework designed to modernize bulk product sales in supermarket and retail environments.

The framework uses controlled dispensing systems to improve customer experience, reduce packaging dependency and increase supply chain resilience.

## Initial Use Case

Bulk Legume Distribution

## Core Objectives

* Improve customer purchasing flexibility
* Reduce packaging dependency
* Increase category sales
* Improve retail differentiation
* Enhance supply chain resilience

## Target Organizations

* Retail Chains
* Supermarkets
* FMCG Retailers
* Food Distribution Networks

## Key Concepts

* Bulk Retail
* Smart Dispensing Systems
* Retail Innovation
