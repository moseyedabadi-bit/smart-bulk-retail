# Smart Bulk Dispenser - Product Vision

## Mission

To transform bulk food retail from a manual and operationally inefficient process into a scalable, data-driven and customer-friendly retail platform.

## Vision

A modular dispensing platform capable of supporting multiple product categories across large retail networks.

## Product Evolution

Level 1:
Manual gravity dispensing.

Level 2:
Smart dispensing with weight sensing and barcode generation.

Level 3:
Full retail integration with POS systems and inventory analytics.

## Future Categories

* Legumes
* Rice
* Coffee
* Nuts
* Grains
* Spices

## Long-Term Goal

Create a smart bulk retail ecosystem deployable across thousands of retail locations.
