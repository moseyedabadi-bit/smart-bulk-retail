# Smart Bulk Dispenser Architecture

## Level 1 - Manual

Components:

* Gravity Hopper
* Manual Lever
* Collection Container

Capabilities:

* Low Cost
* Fast Deployment
* No Electronics

---

## Level 2 - Semi-Automatic

Components:

* Gravity Hopper
* Load Cell
* Weight Display
* Barcode Generator

Capabilities:

* Weight Measurement
* Faster Checkout
* Improved Inventory Accuracy

---

## Level 3 - Fully Automated

Components:

* Gravity Hopper
* Sensors
* POS Integration
* Inventory Management
* Transaction Recording

Capabilities:

* Automated Checkout
* Real-Time Inventory
* Retail Analytics

---

## Design Principles

* Food Grade Materials
* Modular Construction
* Easy Replenishment
* Customer-Friendly Operation
* Scalable Deployment
