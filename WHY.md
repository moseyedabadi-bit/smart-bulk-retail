# Why Smart Bulk Retail?

Retail stores continue to rely heavily on pre-packaged products.

While packaging improves logistics, it also introduces:

* Packaging costs
* Reduced customer flexibility
* Supply chain rigidity
* Increased waste

Smart Bulk Retail explores an alternative retail model where customers can purchase the exact quantity they need while retailers gain operational flexibility and differentiation.

The project aims to combine retail innovation, customer experience and supply chain resilience in a single scalable framework.
